03 — The Studio

About

Meridian Studio is a small architecture visualization practice. We take a firm's plans and elevations and rebuild them as interactive 3D models — so a client can rotate a tower, strip it to wireframe, drag the sun across the sky, and isolate a single floor, all before construction begins.

We work directly with architects during design development, using the same model for internal review, planning submissions, and client presentations — one source of geometry, many audiences.
